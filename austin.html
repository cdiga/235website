Austin, Texas

Being the capital of Texas, Austin is home to many well-known activities. One activity that they are known for is being the Live Music Capital of the World by having many live music concerts every week. The capital building is aso one of the most distingusihed state capitols and is the largest capital in the country. The city of Austin, as well as the whole state, is sunny of over 300 days each year with July being the warmest month.
